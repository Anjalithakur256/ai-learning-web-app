AI Learning Guide is a structured and interactive web platform designed to help learners master Artificial Intelligence step-by-step. The platform provides curated roadmaps, interactive quizzes, progress tracking, and an AI-powered assistant to ensure concept clarity and continuous improvement.

Unlike scattered learning resources, AI Learning Guide organizes AI topics into progressive tracks covering AI Basics, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP, and Generative AI. Each track is carefully structured to move from foundational understanding to real-world implementation.

The platform integrates Firebase for authentication and data management, and Gemini AI for intelligent assistance and NLP-based guidance. It is optimized for performance and deployed using Vercel's lightweight serverless architecture.
